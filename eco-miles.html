EARN ECO-MILES

YOUR CURRENCY TO AFFECT GOOD

Worthy causes are all around us. Often we don't take the time to explore. Or we're unsure whether our donation will be put to good use. Bike & Saddle's Ecomiles Program addresses both these constraints. On each of our journeys, you'll encounter initiatives chosen and supported by our Partners to benefit the community. Examples include a kindergarten, conservation, and a youth bike program in a township.

ONE ECO-MILE

EQUIVALENT TO ONE US$ DOLLAR
  • Join a BIKE & SADDLE Trip

    Eco-miles is your currency to affect good. Earn it on every Bike & Saddle Journey, with every mile you bike, hike or paddle.

  • Earn Eco-Miles FOR EVERY MILE

    For every mile our guests cycle, hike or paddle on a Bike & Saddle Journey, they earn one eco-mile or the equivalent of one US Dollar.

  • SELECT A BENEFICIARY

    Eco-miles can only be donated. Choose from a number of local beneficiary initiatives encountered during your travels or choose to support Big Life.

  • The Big Life Foundation

    For every mile our guests cycle, hike or paddle, Bike &Saddle donates one US dollar to Ride4Lions.
